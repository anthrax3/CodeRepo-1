Duo Runner

A Screen Sharing Multiplayer game in JavaScript.

Description

Duo Runner is a screen sharing multiplayer game written in JavaScript. It's a game in which there a two players at a time who have to cover the obstacles and reach the goal. The screen gets splitted accoding to the player movements.

The Player Controls are given below

Player Controls Function
Red UP Jump
Red DOWN Crouch
Red RIGHT Right
Red LEFT Left
Red UP Jump
Blue W Jump
Blue S Crouch
Blue D Right
Blue A Left
Universal SPACE Restart
Universal ESC Exit
